Overview

Shadow Queen Chess is standard chess with one secret twist: before play begins, each player secretly designates one of their pieces (not the King or Queen) as their Shadow Queen. That piece moves normally — but can also move and capture as a queen at any time.

Activating Your Shadow Queen

At the start of the game, click the ⬡ Activate Shadow Queen button, then click any of your pieces (except the King or Queen) to secretly designate it. A purple dot appears on your piece — only you can see it.

Reveal Conditions

Your Shadow Queen stays hidden until one of these occurs:

  • It moves in a way its original piece couldn't — for example, a Shadow Knight sliding diagonally.
  • Its position puts the opponent's King in check via queen-range that the original piece couldn't explain.

Upon reveal, the piece permanently converts to a queen on the board.

Gambling Rules

  • Each game starts with blinds posted from starting chips. White posts the Big Blind (20%), Black the Small Blind (10%).
  • After each chess move, the mover may place a bet (up to the Big Blind amount), or skip.
  • The opponent must respond: Call, Raise (max 2 raises per round), or Fold & Resign.
  • Folding forfeits the chess game. The chess winner takes the pot. Draws split it.

Winning

Standard chess rules apply: checkmate, timeout, stalemate, or threefold repetition. Resign by folding a bet. The Shadow Queen adds bluff, deduction, and surprise.

How It Connects

This game uses peer-to-peer WebRTC (via PeerJS) — no server stores your game. White hosts and shares a link; Black joins with one click. Works on any modern browser. Both players must stay online for the connection to persist.
