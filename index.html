Shadow Queen Gambling

Two players, one device. Pass it back and forth — no peeking.

The Shadow Queen

Before the game begins, each player secretly selects one of their pieces (not King or Queen) to become their Shadow Queen. This piece has the hidden power to move like a Queen.

Shadow Queen Movement

  • A Shadow Queen can move as either its original piece type or as a Queen on any turn.
  • If it moves in a way that only a Queen could (not its original type), it is instantly revealed — and becomes a permanent Queen.
  • If it checks the King using Queen-range movement that its original type couldn't, it is also revealed.
  • Revealed Shadow Queens always move as Queens from that point on.

Pass & Play

  • After each move, a screen will prompt you to hand the device to your opponent.
  • The pass screen hides the board — tap "Show Board" only when you're holding the device.
  • Neither player can see the other's Shadow Queen dot until it is revealed.

Gambling Rules

  • Each game starts with blinds posted from starting chips. White posts the Big Blind (20%), Black the Small Blind (10%).
  • After each chess move, the mover may place a bet (up to the Big Blind amount), or skip.
  • The opponent must respond: Call, Raise (max 2 raises per round), or Fold & Resign.
  • Folding forfeits the chess game. The chess winner takes the pot. Draws split it.
  • Max bet per offer: equal to the Big Blind.

Standard Chess Rules

Otherwise standard chess applies — en passant, castling, pawn promotion, check, checkmate, stalemate, and threefold repetition all work as normal.
